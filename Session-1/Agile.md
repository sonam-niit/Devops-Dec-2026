# Agile Model

- It is a project management and software development approach
- iterative development (build small pieces of cycle execution)
- Flexible (respond to change, not to stick on rigid plan)
- Colloboration (close team work with developers, stackholders and customers)
- working software delivered

## Agile Manifesto core Values

1. Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
2. Working software over comprehensive documentation
3. Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
4. Responding to change over following a plan

## Agile Frameworks

1. Scrum: Most Popular Framework
2. Kanban
3. XP
4. Lean

### Scrum Framework

- It is very popular and powerfull frameworks used now a days
- simple to manage projects into small units (sprint)
- very easy to understand and execute

**Scrum Roles**

1. Product Owner
    - represent the voice of customer
    - create and manage product req (backlogs)
    - priotize task

2. SM (Scrum Master)
    - coach for a team
    - ensuring best practices followed or not
    - remove blockers for the team
    - protect the team fro outside distractions

3. Development Team

    - cross functional (developers, designers, testers)
    - 5 - 9 members
    - self organized team, build features as per their desicions.



### Events

1. Sprint: 
    - finish authentiction module within 2 weeks
    - which is called sprint 1
2. Sprint Planning:
    - login module (2 days)
    - register module (2 days)
    - forgot password (3 days)
    - chnage password (3 days)
3. Daily Scrum:
    - connect with team daily for 15 or less than 15 minutes
    - give quick updates like
    - what you did yesterday
    - what will you do today
    - is there any roadblock?

4. Sprint Review
    - end of sprint
    - after 2 weeks we will do a small presentation
    - check created stuff
    - take a feedback

5. Sprint Retrospective:
    - team member will write a report
    - what went well? - clint like design, layout, color combination
    - any changes? - 
    - any specific feedback? -
    - what improovements required to do in next sprint
